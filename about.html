About Company

Company "Lefro" - is one of the largest and oldest car-manufacturing companies in Germany. We are manufacturing a wide range of cars for more than hundred years. Our range can suit various demands of our customers. Our cars are manufactured in accordance with the highest international standards. They are sold in different countries of the world. The motors manufactured by our company are used not only in the automobile industry. We also deliver them for industrial, agricultural and scientific purposes. The most of our customers are pleased with the design and technical characteristics of our products.
